Kategorie Formel
Ideales Gasgesetz \( pV = nRT \)
1. Hauptsatz der Thermodynamik \( \Delta U = Q - W \)
Innere Energie eines idealen Gases \( U = \frac{3}{2} nRT \)
Wärmekapazität (konstantes Volumen) \( C_V = \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial T}\right)_V = \frac{3}{2} nR \)
Wärmekapazität (konstanter Druck) \( C_p = \left(\frac{\partial H}{\partial T}\right)_p = \frac{5}{2} nR \)
Entropieänderung \( \Delta S = \int \frac{\delta Q_\text{rev}}{T} \)
Freie Energie (Helmholtz) \( F = U - TS \)
Freie Enthalpie (Gibbs) \( G = H - TS \)
Enthalpie \( H = U + pV \)
Maxwell-Beziehungen \[ \left(\frac{\partial T}{\partial V}\right)_S = -\left(\frac{\partial p}{\partial S}\right)_V \] \[ \left(\frac{\partial T}{\partial p}\right)_S = \left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial S}\right)_p \] \[ \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial V}\right)_T = \left(\frac{\partial p}{\partial T}\right)_V \] \[ \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial p}\right)_T = -\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_p \]
